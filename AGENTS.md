## Code Style and Rules

### Should we maintain backward compatibility, or not?

- Generally, no. Rather than obsessing over backward compatibility or fallback paths, or bloating and cluttering the codebase with overly defensive code, we should prioritize simplicity and cleanliness.
- Be defensive toward the future, not the past.
- Understand that the very fact that you, an AI Agent, are being asked to write code means we are looking toward the future, not the past.
